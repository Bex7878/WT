Creation of the Universe

In the very beginning, before the existence of time, space or reality there was only the nothingness of the Void, a vast emptiness that acted as the home to entities without form or sentience known as the Watchers. However, this silent nothingness was consumed by the sudden appearance of reality and with it the Multiverse, including Runeterra Prime. With this "breath of creation" came the very first Celestials; powerful and enigmatic beings hailing from the newly formed Celestial Realm. These Celestials came in many forms and some were formless, requiring a vessel to contain them. An example of this is the mysterious Bard who possesses a collection of trinkets. Other Celestials took the form of Celestial Dragons such as Aurelion Sol and Inviolus Vox. Aurelion Sol began populating the empty space around him by forging stars. From first these stars, small beings called Meeps were formed from the "residual inspiration and thought" that went into forming them. The exact mechanism that grants these Meeps life is known exclusively by Bard, whom they naturally follow on his travels. While forging the stars, Aurelion Sol would also connect some of his creations to form constellations, being capable of granting some of them life and essentially making Celestials in the form of living constellations.
